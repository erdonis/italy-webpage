Top Destinations on Italy

Rome
The city is a globally renowned cultural and historical powerhouse.

Venice
Its uniqueness can largely be attributed to the canals that run through the city like roads.

Amalfi Coast
The region covers more than two dozen miles of coastline and is home to 13 colorful seaside towns.

Milan
Home to Italy's stock exchange and Fashion Week, plus a hot nightlife, Milan is very much like many other major international cities.
